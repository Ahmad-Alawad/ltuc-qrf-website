How does Her Majesty Queen Rania Al Abdullah Scholarship work?

The grant for young Jordanians in Tafilah Governorate aims to fine-tune their skills in the field of software development in order to enhance their employability in the ICT sector.
The pioneering program leverages fast-paced, career-focused and immersive education known for driving tangible results in a short amount of time. Geared towards passionate learners, the program hones the skills required for today’s jobs by providing students with hands-on software development experience in full-stack JavaScript, Python, ASP.NET and Java, as well as beginner-level HTML, CSS and JavaScript courses.
